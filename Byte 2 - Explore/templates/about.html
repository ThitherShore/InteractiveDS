Accountability: About This Data Set

This statistic shows the global market share held by smartphone vendors worldwide, from the fourth quarter of 2009 to the second quarter of 2018. The data set is based on a worldwide survey by IDC taken place between 4th quarter 2009 to 2nd quarter 2018, and this data set was released on July 2018.

Where did this data come from?

The data was surveyed by IDC and was released on July 2018. The data source can be found here.

Who has access to it?

Everyone who has the above url can access the data. It is a public dataset.
